Seven

Rating and Description

"Seven," directed by David Fincher, is a chilling thriller that follows two detectives, Mills and Somerset, played by Brad Pitt and Morgan Freeman, as they hunt a serial killer who uses the seven deadly sins as his modus operandi. The film masterfully builds tension through its dark atmosphere and complex characters, culminating in a shocking and unforgettable finale. With its exploration of morality and the human psyche, "Seven" not only captivates audiences with its suspenseful storytelling but also leaves them questioning the nature of good and evil. The film is a landmark in the thriller genre, showcasing Fincher's unique vision and storytelling prowess.
