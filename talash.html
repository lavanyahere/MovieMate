Talaash: The Answer Lies Within

Rating and Description

Talaash follows Inspector Surjan Shekhawat, played by Aamir Khan, who is investigating the mysterious death of a popular film actor. The case takes him deep into the underbelly of Mumbai, where he encounters various characters, including a mysterious sex worker named Rosie, played by Kareena Kapoor. As Surjan delves deeper into the investigation, he grapples with his own haunting past, including the tragic death of his son, which continues to affect his life and marriage. The film expertly weaves together themes of grief, guilt, and the search for truth, leading to a gripping climax that challenges the characters' realities.
