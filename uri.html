Uri: The Surgical Strike

Rating and Description

"Uri: The Surgical Strike" is a gripping military drama that recreates the events of the 2016 surgical strikes conducted by the Indian Army. Directed by Aditya Dhar, the film offers a detailed account of the mission, focusing on the meticulous planning and execution by a team of brave soldiers. Vicky Kaushal delivers a powerful performance as Major Vihaan Shergill, capturing the intensity and patriotism of the role. The film's realistic action sequences, combined with its patriotic theme, create a stirring cinematic experience. "Uri" is both a tribute to the valor of the Indian armed forces and a well-crafted action film that keeps viewers on the edge of their seats.
