Wake Up Sid

Rating and Description

"Wake Up Sid" is a heartwarming coming-of-age story directed by Ayan Mukerji. The film follows Sid, a carefree and spoiled young man living in Mumbai, as he gradually learns the value of responsibility and independence. With the help of Aisha, a driven and independent woman he befriends, Sid starts to wake up to the realities of life. Ranbir Kapoor delivers a charming performance as Sid, while Konkona Sen Sharma adds depth to the narrative as Aisha. The film beautifully captures the essence of youth, friendship, and the journey to self-discovery.
