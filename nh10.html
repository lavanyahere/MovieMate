NH10

Rating and Description

"NH10" is a tense thriller that follows Meera, portrayed by Kriti Sanon, and her husband, who encounter a horrifying situation during a road trip. As they drive along the NH10 highway, they witness a brutal crime and become embroiled in a web of violence and retribution. The film delves into themes of gender violence and societal norms, making it a gripping and eye-opening watch. With strong performances and a heart-pounding narrative, "NH10" keeps viewers on the edge of their seats and highlights the darker side of human nature.
