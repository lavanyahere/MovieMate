Extraction

Rating and Description

"Extraction" is an intense action-thriller that follows the story of Tyler Rake, a fearless black-market mercenary, as he embarks on a dangerous mission to rescue the kidnapped son of an international crime lord. The film delivers high-octane action sequences, with impressive stunts and a relentless pace that keeps viewers on the edge of their seats. While the plot may be straightforward, Chris Hemsworth’s powerful performance and the film’s gritty realism make it an exhilarating watch for action fans.
