We're the Millers

Rating and Description

"We're the Millers" is a laugh-out-loud comedy that delivers consistent humor and fun. The film stars Jason Sudeikis as a small-time pot dealer forced to create a fake family to smuggle drugs from Mexico. Jennifer Aniston shines as the no-nonsense stripper-turned-mom, while Will Poulter and Emma Roberts add to the hilarity as the "kids."

The film succeeds in being a light-hearted road-trip comedy with unexpected chemistry between the cast. Though the plot may be predictable, the comedic timing, especially from Sudeikis and Aniston, keeps the audience entertained from start to finish.
