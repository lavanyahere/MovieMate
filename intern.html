The Intern

Rating and Description

"The Intern" is a charming and heartwarming comedy that tells the story of a 70-year-old widower (Robert De Niro) who becomes an intern at an online fashion site run by a high-powered and busy CEO (Anne Hathaway). The film beautifully blends humor with a touching exploration of relationships, mentorship, and personal growth. De Niro's performance as the wise and kind Ben is simply delightful, and Hathaway brings depth to her character as the driven but overwhelmed boss.

The movie offers plenty of laughs, but it's the heartfelt moments and the excellent chemistry between the leads that really stand out. A great feel-good movie that leaves you smiling!
