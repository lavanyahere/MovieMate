Speed

Rating and Description

"Speed" is a high-octane thriller that keeps you on the edge of your seat from start to finish. The film follows a Los Angeles cop who must prevent a bomb from exploding on a city bus by keeping its speed above 50 mph. With non-stop action and thrilling set pieces, "Speed" delivers an adrenaline rush that’s both exciting and intense. The chemistry between the lead actors and the suspenseful plot make it a standout action film of its time.
