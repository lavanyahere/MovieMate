Crazy, Stupid, Love

Rating and Description

"Crazy, Stupid, Love" is a romantic comedy directed by Glenn Ficarra and John Requa. The film tells the story of Cal Weaver, played by Steve Carell, whose life takes an unexpected turn after his wife, played by Julianne Moore, asks for a divorce. With the help of Jacob Palmer, a suave bachelor portrayed by Ryan Gosling, Cal embarks on a journey of self-discovery and re-enters the dating scene. As he navigates love and relationships, the film weaves together multiple storylines, showcasing the complexities of love and the idea that life can be wonderfully chaotic. The film is both hilarious and heartfelt, featuring a talented ensemble cast.
